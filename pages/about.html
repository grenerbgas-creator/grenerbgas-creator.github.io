About Grener Bio

Making clean energy accessible to every Indian home and business since 2015.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify biogas technology by providing a portable, government-approved, and self-serviceable kit. We are committed to lowering the barrier to renewable energy, enabling anyone with a basic HDPE tank and local materials to build their own efficient biogas system and actively participate in a circular economy.

Our Vision

To empower every household and community to transform organic waste into a source of renewable energy, creating a world where clean, self-sufficient, and sustainable living is accessible to all.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

Committed to environmental impact through every installation.

Integrity

Honest advice, transparent pricing, and quality products.

Innovation

Continuous R&D to improve efficiency and user experience.

Community

Building a network of clean energy champions across India.
